Contributions to Established Packages

⚗️

TURBOMOLE

Developed and implemented DFT-based embedding (frozen density and projection-based) coupled with wavefunction theory (WFT) and real-time TD-DFT within the RIPER module. Enables accurate excited-state and adsorption energy calculations for molecule-in-periodic systems at reduced cost.
